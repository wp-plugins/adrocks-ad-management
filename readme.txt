Control your advertisement at your blog.

== Description ==

With Adrocks Ad Management Plugin you can control your Ads on your Blog. 
You can place as many Ad’s in your pages as you want or in the Widget-Bar. 
You also can decide if your Authors can place there Ads on their written Articles and can control 
how often their Ads is shown instead of yours, so you have the full control.


Features:
•	Place Ad where you want in your Pages
•	Place Ad in the Widget-Bar
•	Let your Authors place there Ad at their articels if you want
•	Control how often the Authros Ad is shown in stead of your Ad

Future features:
•	Click-Fraud protection

== Installation ==

1. Upload `adrocks-ad-management-plugin` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Insert your Ad-Code in the Code section
